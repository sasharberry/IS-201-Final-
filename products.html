Growth through the years Sasha Berry Photography & Videography

Sasha Rowberry's journey in photography and videography has afforded her numerous opportunities to collaborate with diverse individuals, continually learn from her experiences, and apply her newfound knowledge. From capturing precious family moments to documenting the joy of newborns, the playfulness of pets, the precious moments of engagements, and the pride of senior graduations, Sasha has embraced a wide range of subjects. Each encounter has provided her with invaluable insights, allowing her to refine her craft and enhance her skills. Sasha's dedication to improvement is evident as she consistently seeks out ways to elevate her work, ensuring that she delivers exceptional results to her clients.

Videography Travel Vlogs

Sasha Rowberry's passion for videography blossomed during her travels, where she discovered the power of capturing moments, weaving them together, and creating compelling narratives filled with emotion and depth. As she ventured to new destinations, Sasha embraced the opportunity to document her experiences through videos, allowing her to showcase the beauty and significance of each place she visited. This process sparked a desire within her to expand her knowledge and skills in videography further. Today, Sasha continues to nurture her love for the art form by consistently creating personal vlogs that chronicle her own journey and sharing event videos that meet the unique needs and desires of her clients. Through her work, she strives to evoke genuine emotions and tell captivating stories that resonate with viewers and leave a lasting impact.

Videography Drone Footage

Sasha Rowberry's videography skills have soared to new heights as she expanded her horizons, quite literally, by exploring aerial videography. Venturing beyond the boundaries of traditional ground-level shots, Sasha delved into the world of capturing perspectives from the sky. With meticulous precision and a keen eye for detail, she mastered the art of showcasing breathtaking landscapes and cityscapes from above. This newfound perspective added a whole new dimension to her work, allowing her to create pieces that not only moved people emotionally but also offered a fresh and awe-inspiring outlook. Sasha's commitment to honing her craft and her understanding of how perspective can profoundly impact storytelling has solidified her ability to create videos that leave a lasting impression on her audience.
