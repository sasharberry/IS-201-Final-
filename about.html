An eclectic story of self discovery About Sasha

Sasha Rowberry's life has been a remarkable journey of discovery, growth, and the pursuit of her passions. Born in Arizona, she experienced a childhood marked by constant movement and new beginnings. However, it was her time in Abu Dhabi, UAE, that truly transformed her life and set her on a path of self-discovery and artistic exploration. With her mantra, "Make it Happen," Sasha embraced every opportunity to nurture her love for photography and videography, gaining diverse perspectives along the way. From her mission in Campinas, Brasil, to her current pursuits as a Marketing Student at Brigham Young University, Sasha's story is one of seizing moments and celebrating unique experiences.

Born in Arizona, Sasha Rowberry was introduced to change at an early age. Her family's frequent relocations exposed her to new cultures, people, and environments. These early experiences ignited a sense of adventure within her and fostered an appreciation for diversity that would shape her future endeavors.

It was in Abu Dhabi, UAE, that Sasha truly found herself. Immersed in a vibrant and multicultural society, she encountered a world of possibilities for her photography and videography pursuits. The city became her muse, offering a wide range of perspectives and unique stories waiting to be captured. Sasha discovered her own style and voice as she embraced the opportunities that Abu Dhabi provided.

Unleashing Creativity Through Client Work With her growing love for photography and videography, Sasha sought to refine her skills by engaging in various client projects. From weddings to corporate events, she explored different aspects of her craft and discovered new facets of her creativity. Each project was a chance to learn, grow, and connect with people through her art.

Sasha's journey took an unexpected turn when she served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Campinas, Brasil. Even in this religious context, she found a way to incorporate her videography skills by serving in a marketing position for the organization. Through her videos, she shared inspiring messages and stories, touching the lives of many.

Currently a Marketing Student at Brigham Young University, Sasha Rowberry continues to chase her dreams with unwavering determination. The academic environment provides her with a platform to further refine her skills and expand her understanding of marketing strategies. Sasha is eager to apply her unique perspectives and experiences gained from her global encounters in her future professional endeavors.

Throughout her journey, Sasha remains grateful for the incredible influences and unique experiences she has had with people from all over the world. The friendships forged, the cultures encountered, and the lessons learned have all contributed to her personal and artistic growth. Sasha Rowberry embraces the opportunities that lie ahead and is ready to continue making her mark in the world of photography, videography, and marketing.

Sasha Rowberry's life story is a testament to the power of embracing change, seizing opportunities, and staying true to one's passions. From her nomadic upbringing to her transformative experiences in Abu Dhabi, Sasha has nurtured her love for photography and videography while uncovering her own style and voice. As she continues her studies and explores new avenues in the field of marketing, Sasha remains grateful for the remarkable influences and experiences that have shaped her journey. With her mantra, "Make it Happen," Sasha Rowberry is poised to make a lasting impact through her art and leave an indelible mark on the world.
